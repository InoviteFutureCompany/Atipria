Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for new users before they install Atipria.

How accurate is Atipria?

Atipria analyzes dozens of signals — including metadata, lighting consistency, compression artifacts, source information and AI patterns. Instead of relying on a single score, it provides a confidence assessment to help you make informed decisions.

Does Atipria store my data?

No. Privacy comes first. Atipria is designed for secure analysis and does not sell or share your personal data.

Does it work on every website?

Atipria supports most major websites and social media platforms. Support for additional websites is continuously added through product updates.

Can Atipria detect AI images, videos and audio?

Yes. Atipria is designed to analyze AI-generated images, manipulated videos, cloned voices and other suspicious digital media.

Is Atipria free?

Yes. The Free Plan includes basic deepfake detection, limited image recognition and 1 device. Premium, Business and Enterprise plans unlock stronger protection and more devices.

How long does an analysis take?

Most analyses complete within 2–5 seconds, depending on the media type, quality and complexity.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome is fully supported today. Additional browser support is planned for future releases.

Can I trust the results?

No AI detector is perfect. Atipria is built to assist your judgment by combining multiple verification methods instead of relying on a single AI model.
